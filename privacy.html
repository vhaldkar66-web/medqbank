Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

MedQBank ("we", "our", or "us") operates the website medqbank.in. This page explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our site.

1. Information We Collect

Data you provide: We do not require account registration. No personal information (name, email, phone) is required to use the QBank.

Automatically collected data: When you visit MedQBank, we may automatically collect:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website
  • Device type and screen resolution
  • IP address (anonymised)

Local storage: Your quiz progress, answered questions, and bookmarks are stored in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not sent to our servers.

2. Cookies

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Store your cookie consent preference. Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics): Help us understand how users interact with the site. Only set if you accept cookies.
  • Advertising cookies (Google AdSense): Used to show relevant advertisements. Only set if you accept cookies.

You can reject non-essential cookies at any time using the cookie banner or by clearing your browser data.

3. Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our site or other sites. You can opt out of personalised advertising at google.com/settings/ads.

For more information on how Google uses data, see Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymised usage data. We have enabled IP anonymisation. Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Third-Party Links

MedQBank contains affiliate links to third-party platforms (Marrow, PrepLadder, DAMS). These sites have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for their practices.

6. Children's Privacy

MedQBank is intended for medical students (adults aged 18+). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal profile to access, correct or delete. To clear your quiz progress, use the "Reset Session" button in the QBank. To clear analytics cookies, use your browser's privacy settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
