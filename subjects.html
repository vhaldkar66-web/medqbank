Frequently Asked Questions

Which exams are covered in MedQBank?
MedQBank covers NEET PG (2018–2024), AIIMS PG (2017–2020), INICET (2020–2024), FMGE/MCI Screening (2020–2025), and UPSC CMS (2023) — a total of 8,379 questions.
Do I need to create an account?
No. MedQBank is completely free and requires no login. Your progress and bookmarks are saved automatically in your browser.
Which subject has the most questions?
Medicine has the most questions (1,606), followed by Psychiatry (1,106), Preventive & Social Medicine (1,044), FMGE (2,811 total), and Pharmacology (648).
Can I filter questions by year?
Yes. In the QBank, use the Year filter in the sidebar to show questions from a specific exam year, from 2017 to 2025.
What is Practice Mode vs Test Mode?
Practice Mode automatically shows the explanation as soon as you select an answer — ideal for active learning. Test Mode hides the explanation until you click "Explanation", letting you attempt the question first and self-assess.
Are these the actual exam questions?
These are recalled questions from previous year papers as reported by students and published by coaching institutes. They may differ slightly from officially released papers.
