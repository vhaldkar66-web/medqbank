About MedQBank

MedQBank is a free, open-access platform for medical students preparing for postgraduate entrance examinations in India. We provide previous year MCQs from NEET PG, AIIMS PG, INICET, FMGE (MCI Screening Test), and UPSC CMS — organised by subject and topic, with detailed explanations.

Our Mission

Quality medical PG preparation resources should be accessible to every student — not just those who can afford expensive subscriptions. MedQBank was built to give every MBBS student free access to a comprehensive, well-organised question bank.

What We Offer

  • 8,379 previous year MCQs from 2017 to 2025
  • Subjectwise and topicwise organisation across 20 subjects
  • Detailed explanations for every question
  • Practice Mode and Test Mode
  • Bookmark system and session tracking (no login required)
  • Filter by exam type (NEET PG, AIIMS, INICET, FMGE, UPSC CMS) and year

Disclaimer

The questions on MedQBank are recalled from previous year papers as published by coaching institutes and student communities. They are for educational purposes only and may not be verbatim reproductions of official exam papers. See our full Medical Disclaimer.
